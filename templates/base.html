ABOUT

The project aims to introduce Sudanese heritage and provide historical data about people, places and tools related to Sudanese heritage. If you find an image and do not know the people/building tools in it, upload it and the artificial intelligence algorithm will find answers to your questions.

FAQ

How can I find information about a person/tool/structure that I have an image of? Upload that image using above upload feature, if it is in our database, the information about will appear to you. . . What if the image is not in the database? If you know any information about the image, please attach it, our team will verify the information and update database. . . Can I delete an Image that I uploaded? Contact us using admin@sudaneseheritage.net and we will delete it based on your request.
